About TomKim3dprints

Maker. Creator. Innovator.

Hi, I'm Tom

Welcome to TomKim3dprints — a passion project turned small business. I've been 3D printing for years, turning digital designs into physical reality. From intricate miniatures to functional mechanical parts, I love the challenge of every new print.

Based out of Lakeland, Florida, I ship nationwide and offer custom design services for clients who want something truly unique. Whether it's a one-off art piece or a batch of production parts, I bring the same attention to detail to every job.
